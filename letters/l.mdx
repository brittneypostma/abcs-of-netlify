With [Netlify Labs](https://app.netlify.com/user/labs) anyone can log into Netlify and turn beta features on and off with the click of a button. If you have a free account, head over to [app.netlify.com/user/labs](http://app.netlify.com/user/labs) to get started testing things like Netlify Graph, scheduled functions, scoped environment variables, and more!
